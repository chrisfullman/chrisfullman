About Me

I'm a member of the Board of Directors for Girls For A Change, a nonprofit organization in Central Virginia that serves girls of color in elementary through high school and beyond. We provide STEAM-focused training, certifications, networking opportunities, soft skill coaching, paid internships, and more.

Outside of Girls For A Change, I run my own digital marketing studio, Antimatter Co., offering WordPress development, maintenance, and security services for nonprofits, agencies, and small-to-Fortune 500 organizations.
