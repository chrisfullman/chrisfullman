About Chris

Chris Fullman is a veteran of the web industry and has 20 of agency experience―notably with TEN (formerly AgencyNet) in South Florida, and The Martin Agency in Richmond, VA―under his belt.

From full-time development to leading development teams, Chris has been fortunate to work with some of the best marketers and production teams around. His work with these agencies and teams have been recognized and awarded within the industry and shown in college classes as case studies for digital marketing.

In 2019, Chris graduated from Leadership Metro Richmond's Leadership Quest program. He aims to take his experience and find ways to better serve nonprofits, advocacy groups, local governments, and community organizations.

Currently, Chris is member on the Board of Directors for Girls For A Change, a nonprofit youth development organization aimed at empowering girls of color in Central Virginia to visualize theirs bright futures and potential through discovery, development, innovation and social change in their communities.
